What is a Photo Book?

@@include('../i/resp-img2.html',{ src:'photo-book', type:'jpg', alt:'Photo Book example showing image of dogs', priority:'1', width:'938', height:'526', folder:'4.1-photo-book-vs-photo-album', })

A photo book is digitally printed on flexible pages and is glue bound like a traditional book. Photo book pages do not lay flat when open: there is a gutter between the two pages on a spread, so if a photo crosses over two pages you can lose a bit in the gutter. Photo books are sturdy, can accommodate up to 240 pages and make for the ideal coffee table photo book.

What is a Layflat Photo Album?

@@include('../i/resp-img2.html',{ src:'layflat-photobook', type:'jpg', alt:'Open layflat photo book showing uninterrupted double page spread', priority:'1', width:'938', height:'526', folder:'4.1-photo-book-vs-photo-album', })

Our Layflat Photo Albums are developed on silver halide photo paper, which offers the colors and brilliance of a genuine photo print. These albums are bound using a special technique that allows the pages to lay flat when open. These pages are much thicker and stiffer than a photo book, but not as thick or rigid as a premium layflat album. The uninterrupted spreads make lay flat photo albums ideal for weddings or other special occasions.
